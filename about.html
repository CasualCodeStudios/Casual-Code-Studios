💡 How We Started

Born out of late nights, keyboard clacks, and a burning curiosity about how websites actually work — Casual Code Studios started as one person's obsession with making things look great on screens.

2024
Founded in Nyeri

Building the Web,
One Pixel at a Time

We started Casual Code Studios with one belief: great websites shouldn't be the exclusive domain of big-budget companies. Small businesses, artists, farmers, entrepreneurs — everyone deserves a web presence that looks like it cost millions.

Today, we're a small but mighty frontend agency that punches well above our weight class. We build e-commerce stores, landing pages, portfolios, and full business websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a healthy dose of AI-assisted development.

We also believe in giving back to the community — which is why we run beginner-friendly web development courses for anyone who wants to learn the craft.
